Clever Twit

Clever Twit is an intelligent hashtag recommendation tool for use with Twitter, that makes it easier for you to compose tweets with relevant hashtags, as well as aiding your search and navigation of Twitter as a whole.

Clicking the compose button next to your username in the top right will allow you to write tweets using intelligent hashtag suggestions.

You can use the search button on the navbar at the top to perform a context-aware hashtag search of Twitter.
